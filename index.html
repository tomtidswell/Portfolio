My Story

I’m Tom, a software analyst, turned software engineer from Yorkshire. I began my interest in tech fifteen years ago, with the basics of general programming and internet technology.

Thanks to my eleven years experience as a consultant software analyst for IBM, and also freelancing in the same role, I’m someone who can communicate clearly to a non-technical audience.

I pride myself in being a careful thinker and listener with the ability to tackle complex logical challenges. I have worked across Europe in the gas and electricity market, designing and building APIs in enterprise software for customer websites.

To modernise my skills, I attended a bootcamp at General Assembly, which focussed on development with a MERN full stack. I’m looking for opportunities which will broaden my skills, and to work for a company where I can contribute and make a real difference.
